About Organizers

Being a Cosmos community member since early 2021 and a founder of ch0pch0p NFT, Thang recognized that Cosmos has an organic community but lacks a centralized party to coordinate things and unite people.

Hence, we decided to organize the first virtual Cosmos NFT Conference. This is a place for like-minded people to share their thoughts, recognize their contributions and also for NFT projects to showcase their works.

Mint and trade NFTs on the fastest community-owned app chain, secured and governed by $STARS.
